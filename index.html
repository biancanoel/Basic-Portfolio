About Me

Hi there, friends! A little about myself... It took a while for me to realize that I was interested in anything STEM related. I had always shyed away because I assumed it would be too hard. It wasn't till my advanced math and stats courses at UC Davis while pursuing a BS in Managerial Economics that I realized I enjoyed solving hard, technical problems and considered switching After working a few years at a SAAS start up, including as a product manager, I was exposed to developers and knew this was the challenge that would keep my engaged and continously learning.

My main personal interests are outdoor activities and my dog. The best is outdoor activities that my dog can be part of, like hiking, tennis (she's a great ball fetcher), and going to the beach! The end of the day ideally ends in a great food and wine with a small group of friends. But be warned, I turn into a grumpy troll after 11 PM! The earlier I am in bed the better.
